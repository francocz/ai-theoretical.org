Invitation to Participate

AI-Theoretical invites researchers and scholars interested in using AI as an active partner in theoretical work to participate in the project.

The platform hosts high-level preprints or working papers developed in explicit collaboration with AI systems. Contributions may come from different fields, including the sciences, mathematics, philosophy, and other domains where theoretical reasoning plays a central role.

Texts that fit the scope of the platform may subsequently undergo an AI-performed editorial assessment, carried out in accordance with the provisions outlined in the AI editorial prompt and published alongside the paper.

If you are interested in contributing or in discussing a possible collaboration, you are invited to get in touch.

Contact: franco.cazzaniga@uninsubria.it
