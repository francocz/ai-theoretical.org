Founding Paper

The Invisible Pillar: On the Genesis and Concealment of Dualism from the Pre-Socratics to the Moderns
December 2025
Franco Cazzaniga & Claude Opus 4.5 (Anthropic)
Abstract: This paper investigates the structural persistence of dualism within Western metaphysical thought, tracing its genealogy from the fragments of the Pre-Socratics to its formal consolidation in the early modern period. It argues that the mind-body distinction functions as an "invisible pillar" that simultaneously supports and remains concealed within the framework of modern scientific rationality. By leveraging AI co-authorship, the study maps conceptual recurrences across vast philosophical corpora, offering a novel synthesis of historical analysis and computational epistemology.
