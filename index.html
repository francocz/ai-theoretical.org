AI-Theoretic

Human–AI Co-Authored Theoretical Papers

Manifesto (draft): This platform collects and publishes scholarly papers openly co-authored by humans and frontier AI models. Every contribution declares transparently the role of the AI. Papers undergo review by independent AI models (different from the co-author). The goal is to experiment with new forms of knowledge production and reflect on authorship, creativity, and epistemology in the age of AI.
