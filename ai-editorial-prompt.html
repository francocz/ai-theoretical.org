AI Editorial Prompts

Here you find the prompts used to ask the AI to assess papers before they are published on this site. Each submission track has its own prompt with specific evaluation criteria.

When a paper satisfies these criteria, the resulting AI-assisted editorial assessment is published alongside the text. Making both the prompts and the assessments public guarantees transparency and allows the evaluation process to be repeated, within the limits imposed by differences between AI models and versions.
