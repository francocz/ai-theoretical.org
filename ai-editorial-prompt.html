AI Editorial Prompt

Here you find the prompt used to ask the AI to assess papers before they are published on this site. The prompt specifies how the AI evaluates a text with respect to the platform's editorial criteria.

When a paper satisfies these criteria, the resulting AI-assisted editorial assessment is published alongside the text. Making both the prompt and the assessment public guarantees transparency and allows the evaluation process to be repeated, within the limits imposed by differences between AI models and versions.
