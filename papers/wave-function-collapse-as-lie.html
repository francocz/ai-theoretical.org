Wave Function Collapse as Lie Algebra Contraction: The SU(2) Spin-1/2 Paradigm

Franco Cazzaniga — December 2025

We propose a geometric mechanism for the emergence of discrete measurement outcomes in the Stern–Gerlach experiment, based on the Inönü–Wigner contraction of Lie algebras. The inhomogeneous magnetic field of the apparatus explicitly breaks the internal SU(2) symmetry of the spin degree of freedom, inducing a continuous deformation of the spin algebra toward the Euclidean algebra e(2).
